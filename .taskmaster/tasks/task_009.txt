# Task ID: 9
# Title: Integrate Sound Design with Gameplay
# Status: pending
# Dependencies: 4, 8
# Priority: medium
# Description: Ensure sound design elements are effectively integrated into gameplay.
# Details:
Link sound effects to specific gameplay events, such as footsteps approaching or doorbell rings. Adjust audio levels for optimal player experience.

# Test Strategy:
Test the integration of sound effects during gameplay to ensure they enhance the experience without being distracting.

# Subtasks:
## 1. Link Sound Effects to Gameplay Events [pending]
### Dependencies: None
### Description: Identify specific gameplay events that require sound effects, such as footsteps, doorbell rings, and notifications. Create a mapping of these events to their corresponding sound effects.
### Details:
Use Unity's event system to trigger sound effects when specific gameplay actions occur. Ensure that each sound effect is linked to the appropriate event in the game logic.

## 2. Adjust Audio Levels for Optimal Experience [pending]
### Dependencies: 9.1
### Description: Analyze the audio levels of each sound effect and adjust them to ensure a balanced audio experience for players. This includes ensuring that sound effects are not too loud or too soft compared to the game's background music.
### Details:
Utilize Unity's audio mixer to adjust the volume levels of each sound effect. Test the audio levels in various gameplay scenarios to ensure they enhance the experience without being distracting.

## 3. Implement Dynamic Audio Feedback [pending]
### Dependencies: 9.1, 9.2
### Description: Create a system that dynamically adjusts sound effects based on gameplay context, such as increasing the intensity of footsteps when a player is being pursued.
### Details:
Use Unity's audio system to implement dynamic audio feedback. This may involve changing pitch, volume, or adding effects based on player actions or game states.

## 4. Integrate Sound Design with Gameplay Mechanics [pending]
### Dependencies: 9.1, 9.2, 9.3
### Description: Ensure that sound effects are integrated with core gameplay mechanics, such as player actions and environmental interactions.
### Details:
Review the core gameplay mechanics and identify where sound effects can enhance player interactions. Implement sound triggers for actions like opening doors or receiving notifications.

## 5. Conduct Final Audio Testing and Adjustments [pending]
### Dependencies: 9.1, 9.2, 9.3, 9.4
### Description: Perform comprehensive testing of all sound design elements in the game to ensure they work harmoniously together and contribute to the overall player experience.
### Details:
Gather feedback from playtesters regarding the audio experience. Make necessary adjustments based on feedback to improve sound design integration.
